ACT NOW Education

ACT Now Education provides transitioning service members, veterans, and military spouses with access to free career development resources. Through an initial informational meeting, participants can explore various tools and opportunities designed to support their professional growth.

As part of this initiative, individuals receive access to CareerFlow.AI and Coursera, enabling them to enhance their skills and refine their career strategies. These resources play a vital role in preparing military-affiliated individuals for successful transitions into civilian careers.

To register for a free Splunk account through ACT Now Education, you must first attend a Weekly Rundown session, then sign up for a free account on ACT Now Education and join their Facebook group for updates. Complete the Splunk Registration Form, usually found on their website or shared within the community. After submitting your information, wait for a confirmation email with activation instructions, then log in to Splunk and start learning.

Key Steps:
  • Verify if you meet the Splunk eligibility requirements (e.g., military affiliation, veteran status).
  • Attend a Weekly Rundown session hosted by ACT Now Education.
  • Sign up on ACT Now Education’s website and join their Facebook group.
  • Complete the Splunk Registration Form with required details.
  • Check your email (including spam/junk folders) for confirmation.
  • Activate your Splunk account and access training resources.
  • Reach out for support via ACT Now Education’s website or Facebook group if needed.

American Corporate Partners (ACP)

ACP is a nationwide nonprofit dedicated to helping military service members and veterans successfully transition into civilian careers through personalized, one-on-one mentorship. ACP connects veterans with experienced professionals from Fortune 500 companies, top organizations, and various industries to provide career guidance, resume building, networking advice, and job search strategies.

What sets ACP apart:
  • Free, Customized Mentorship: Veterans are paired with mentors tailored to their career goals, creating personalized guidance over a 12-month period.
  • Industry Expertise: Mentors come from diverse fields, including IT, engineering, finance, and project management, offering insider knowledge.
  • Proven Success: Many veterans attribute job offers and promotions to the connections and advice gained through ACP.

Completing an application takes just a few minutes. Once you apply, an ACP staff member will reach out to you within one business day to schedule a phone call to learn more about your career goals, preferences and background. If you have any questions about our application, please email ACP staff at info@acp-usa.org or call (212) 752-0700.

FourBlock

FourBlock’s Career Readiness Program prepares transitioning service members and veterans for meaningful civilian careers by providing the knowledge, skills, and professional connections needed for success. In partnership with top companies and Columbia University, the program delivers world-class career transition training tailored to the unique challenges veterans face.

Offered twice a year in major cities nationwide, the program includes weekly sessions with industry professionals, hands-on career search training, and direct access to veteran and civilian mentors. Participants receive personalized guidance from volunteer career coaches, helping them refine their career strategies and effectively communicate their military experience.

Graduates leave the program with a clear career action plan, an expanded professional network, and ongoing access to career counseling, networking events, and professional development opportunities. FourBlock ensures that every participant is set up for long-term career success.

What sets FourBlock apart:
  • Employer-Integrated Learning: Weekly sessions with top companies provide direct exposure to corporate culture, hiring practices, and industry insights.
  • Personalized Career Coaching: One-on-one mentorship from veteran alumni and industry professionals ensures tailored career guidance.
  • Academic Partnership: Developed in collaboration with Columbia University, the curriculum is grounded in the latest career and learning research.
  • Lifelong Support & Networking: Graduates gain continued access to career counseling, nationwide networking events, and professional development opportunities.

FourBlock’s Find Your Calling – Online Course helps transitioning service members and veterans identify fulfilling career paths that align with their skills, values, and interests. Designed as a self-paced, interactive program, it provides a structured approach to career exploration and decision-making.

Developed in partnership with Columbia University, the course combines expert insights, guided exercises, and real-world examples to help participants clarify their career goals. Veterans gain access to resources that translate military experience into civilian opportunities and build confidence in their next steps.

By the end of the course, participants have a clear career direction, a personalized action plan, and the tools to successfully navigate their transition into meaningful civilian careers.
