Privacy Act

Last Updated: 11 February 2025

At tahraun.com, I respect your privacy and am committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit my website. 1. Information tahraun.com Collects I only collect email addresses provided voluntarily when you subscribe to my newsletter. 2. How tahraun.com Uses Your Information Your email will only be used to: • Send newsletters with updates on my transition journey, career insights, and useful resources. • Share relevant content to help with military-to-civilian transitions. I will never sell, rent, or share your email with third parties for marketing purposes. 3. Third-Party Services I use GitHub to manage and send newsletters. Your email is securely stored according to their privacy policies. We have not yet begun officially collecting email address or other personally identifiable information (PII), and tahraun.com will not ask for it. If you are being asked to submit your personal information, it should be suspected that the entity is a malicious actor and does not operate as an official member of tahraun.com. 4. Your Rights & Choices • You can unsubscribe anytime using the “Unsubscribe” link in any email. • If you want your email removed from my database, contact me at tahraun.engineering@gmail.com. • If you are a resident of the EU (GDPR compliance) or U.S. (CAN-SPAM Act), you have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your data. 5. Data Protection I take reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or misuse. 6. Future Updates If I add new features (e.g., a contact form, user comments), this policy will be updated accordingly. Any changes will be posted here with a revised date. For any questions or privacy concerns, feel free to reach out at tahraun.engineering@gmail.com.
